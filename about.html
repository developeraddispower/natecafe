Our Story

Born in Addis Ababa, Built with Passion

Nate Café & Restaurant was born from a simple dream: to create a place in Addis Ababa where every guest feels truly at home. Founded over five years ago, we have grown from a cozy neighborhood café into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our founder, inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Ethiopia and the elegance of international cuisine, set out to craft a menu that celebrates both worlds. Every recipe tells a story — of family, heritage, and the joy of sharing a great meal.

Today, Nate Café is more than a restaurant. It's a gathering place for families, friends, business meetings, and milestone celebrations.
